Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Data Collection

This extension does NOT collect any personal data. All settings are stored locally on your device only.

Permissions Used

  • storage: Saves your preferences locally
  • activeTab: Detects game pages to enable the extension
  • host permissions: Allows the extension to work on game websites

Third Parties

No data is shared with third parties. No analytics, no tracking, no advertising.

Contact

For questions, contact via Chrome Web Store support.
